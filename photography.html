Photography;

I picked up photography back in 2012 buying my first Canon Rebel T3, but it never really went anywhere. I took the camera with me everywhere trying to get better but never really educated myself in the ways of a photographer. In the Winter of 2017 I picked it up again and I was hooked.

Right now I shoot with my Canon EOS 6D for my professional work. I've mostly been doing portrait shots with my camera but I have recently started working on some concept photography.
