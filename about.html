Who am I?

My name is Jose F. Hernandez-Uribe and I am a Software Engineer. I graduated with a bachelors in Computer Science at California State University, Long Beach in the Spring of 2019. While at the school I was a part of two honors societies; IEEE-HKN Honor Society and The Honor Society of Phi Kappa Phi.

During University I mainly focused my free time on web development and cyber security. The IOT always had my interest and I continue dedicating my free time to learning web development both front and back-end.

In my senior project course at CSULB, I worked on creating a website with API routes so that an Arduino board and an IOS application could save data on a remote MongoDB database. The website I created also had a web user interface that way the data saved from the Arduino board could be visible on the desktop as well. I plan on working on the site in the future so, don't mind it right now. It's a work in progress.
